## 📝 How to Use This Dictionary

1. **Print or keep open** while recording
2. **Read the Kinyarwanda column** into your microphone
3. **Speak clearly** - one phrase per recording
4. **Record ~5 seconds** for each phrase
5. **Continue for all phrases** you want to cover

**Example Recording Session:**
```
[1/18] Read this sentence:
>>> Murakaza neza.
(Welcome)

Press ENTER when ready to record...
[Record for 5 seconds]

Continue? (y/n): y

[2/18] Read this sentence:
>>> Muraho
(Hello)

Press ENTER when ready to record...
[Record for 5 seconds]

Continue? (y/n): y
```
